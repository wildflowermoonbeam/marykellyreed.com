intuit: intuitive collage practice

2020-2022

Found paper, tape, stickers

The first of these collages appears in a journal from Summer 2020. Its creation was circumstantial: due to supply chain issues caused by Covid, I wasn't able to find the specific composition notebook I prefer to use for journaling, and I'd settled for a 3-subject version of my usual notebook. This version included two yellow kraft paper dividers. I didn't need the dividers for anything functional, so I arranged found paper on the page and glued it into place.

moon
2020 (sometime between May and July)
7.25" x 9.625"

I used the same style of notebook for my next journal. I used the practice as a meditation on composition, and allowed the meanings of each work to come through in the juxtapostion of images and my word choices.
